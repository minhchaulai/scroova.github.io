Club Information

We simulate the United Nations by representing countries and defending their positions on global issues.


school

Skills

We practice and improve our speaking, debating, critical reasoning skills, and the ability to view global issues from an international perspective.

event

Events

Outside from regular meetings, Model UN hosts workshops throughout the year for leadership and speaking. In December, we host our own Model UN conference for high school students around Colorado.

group

Social

Outside of the club, we are a welcoming and inclusive community with diverse backgrounds and majors. We host parties, social events, and group dinners on a regular basis, all of which are completely optional and separate from main club activities.

We attend two conferences per year: Washington, DC in fall, and New York City in spring.

airplanemode_active

National Conferences

The National conferences in DC and NY are an amazing opportunity to truly emulate the United Nations. You get a true, proper experience of representing a country in the UN, and have serious speeches, working papers, and voting sessions that can't be replicated. In addition to that, you get to meet dozens of people from around the whole world, and form friendships that last forever. Plus, you get to have a great time in some of the best cities in the US!

We hope to see you at our meetings!

Meeting Info

Our meetings are held in the Visual Arts Center (VAC) room 1B90 on Thursdays at 6:30pm.

Don't hesitate to contact us with questions at modelun.colorado.edu
